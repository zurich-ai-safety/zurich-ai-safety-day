Swiss AI Safety Days 2026

Code of Conduct

Swiss AI Safety Days is committed to creating an inclusive, respectful, and safe environment for all participants. We believe that diversity of perspectives strengthens our community and advances our shared mission of ensuring AI benefits humanity.

Our Agreement

At the Swiss AI Safety Days all attendees agree to:

  • Respect the boundaries of other participants
  • Look out for one another and try to help if you can
  • Adhere to national and local health and safety regulations
  • Respect diverse perspectives

Professional Standards

This is a professional learning and networking event. These behaviors don't belong at the Swiss AI Safety Days or related events:

  • Unwanted sexual attention or sexual harassment of any kind
  • Using the participant database to request meetings for romantic or sexual reasons
  • Offensive, disruptive, or discriminatory actions or communication

Our Approach

We understand that human interaction is complex. If you feel able, please give each other the benefit of explaining behavior you find unwelcome or offensive.

If you're asked to stop a behavior that's causing a problem for someone, we expect you to stop immediately.

Support

At the event we'll have multiple community contact people whose role is to be available for personal or interpersonal problems that come up. They will be pointed out upon registration and wear badges.

Contact Information

If you need to report an incident or have questions about this Code of Conduct, please contact us:

Email: team@aisafety.ch

During the event: Approach any staff member wearing an official badge
